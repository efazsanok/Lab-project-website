Trees of Bangladesh

Explore the native flora, crops, and timber that keep our country green and healthy.

üìç Common in: Gazipur, Tangail

üìç Common in: Dinajpur, Ishwardi

üìç Common in: Barisal, Jhalokathi

üìç Common in: Khulna, Noakhali, Barisal

üìç Season: Rainy Season
